Abstract
Systems for Ambient Intelligence contexts are expected to exhibit an autonomous and intelligent behavior by understanding and reacting to the activities that take place in such contexts. The work proposed here advocates a common-sense approach as a solution to the shortage of current systems for Ambient Intelligence when dealing with unexpected scenarios.

Global Design Space Explorations Strategy for Systems Synthesis
F. Moya, J.M. Moya Fernández, J.C. López
Cenference: European Concurrent Engineering Conference
Location: Erlangen-Nuremberg (Alemania)
Date: 21/04/1999 - 23/04/1999
Pages: 39-43
Abstract
In this paper we present some experimental data testing robustness of heuristic exploration of the design space in CAD tools. We show the influence of uncertainty and other usual properties in the effectiveness of exploration. We propose a strategy for increased effectiveness that takes advantage of previous knowledge of the design space.

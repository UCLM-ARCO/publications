System Dynamics for Environmental Management: four case studies
I. Fernández Pérez; F. Moya; J.C. González
Cenference: European Simulation Multiconference
Location: Darmstadt (Alemania)
Date: 03/06/2002 - 05/06/2002
Pages: 461-465
Abstract
System Dynamics is a broad spectrum methodology with a great potential for evaluation of environmental policies and for a better undestanding of ecosystems. In this paper we show four case studies addressing some representative problems related to management of natural resources, stressing the strengths and weaknesses if this methodology.
